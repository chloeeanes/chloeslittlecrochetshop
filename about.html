About the Shop Owner

Hello! I'm Chloe! Welcome to my shop :)!

I decided to create this shop for my future products such as crocheted apparel and plushies, as well as printed illustration/paintings.

I have always loved creating art pieces since I was in elementary school and knew I would center my life around arts one day. Well, today's the day...um...after I graduate from uni!!

One day, I will also center my life around web comic creation and will sell print copies of my stories here once I can.

Currently, this shop is not open due to lack of materials and time needed to create these items. I hope to open business when my time at university is over in a year!

If you want to check out more of my work, or have any questions, please send me an email! (I am currently working on creating socials for this shop, please bear with me as this takes time that I don't have right now)
